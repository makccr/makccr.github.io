Workout Protocol

Things to keep in mind:

  • This is designed to be so intentionally easy that you can't fail.
  • DO NOT EXCEED THESE MINIMUM METRICS. It will be easy to, by design, but don't. It's better to leave yourself wanting more.
  • Try your best to ensure you never spend more than 1hr a day total exercising.
  • 10,000 steps a day as a minimum is a good metric to aim for, but do not chase that if it means sacrificing one of the items listed below.

Week 1

  1. Mon, Wed, Fri: 15min fartlek; 1min run / 2min walking
  2. Daily: 5 push-ups, 5 squats, 2 pull-up negative

Week 2

  1. Mon, Wed, Fri: 20min fartlek; 2min run / 2min walking
  2. Daily: 10 push-ups, 10 squats, 5 pull-up negative

Week 3

  1. Mon, Wed, Fri: 30min fartlek; 3min run / 1.5min walking
  2. Daily: 15 push-ups, 15 squats, 7 pull-up negative

Week 4

  1. Mon, Wed, Fri: 30min fartlek; 3min run / 1.5min walking
  2. Daily: 15 push-ups, 15 squats, 7 pull-up negative

Week 5

  1. Mon, Wed, Fri: 25min endurance run (can stop at 2 miles)
  2. Daily: 20 push-ups, 20 squats, 10 pull-up negative

Week 6

  1. Mon, Wed, Fri: 30min endurance run (can stop at 2.5 miles)
  2. Daily: 20 push-ups, 20 squats, 10 pull-up negative

Week 7

  1. Mon, Wed, Fri: 35min endurance run (can stop at 3 miles)
  2. Daily: 25 push-ups, 25 squats, 15 pull-up negative

Week 8

  1. Mon, Wed, Fri: 40min endurance run (can stop at 3.5 miles)
  2. Daily: 30 push-ups, 30 squats, 20 pull-up negative

Week 9

  1. Mon, Wed, Fri: 4 mile endurance run
  2. Daily: 35 push-ups, 35 squats, 5 pull ups

Week 10

  1. Mon, Wed, Fri: 4.5 mile endurance run
  2. Daily: 40 push-ups, 40 squats, 10 pull ups

Week 11

  1. Mon, Wed, Fri: 5 mile endurance run
  2. Daily: 45 push-ups, 45 squats, 15 pull ups

Week 12

  1. Mon, Wed, Fri: 5K time trial - all 3 days
  2. Daily: 50 push-ups, 50 squats, 20 pull ups

Diet Protocol

Things to keep in mind:

  • Measure your weight every morning, but only make a diet change after two-weeks of a new diet.
  • The goal is to start with a 30-day fast - you will fail at this goal.
  • When you fail to to the Diet plan

Diet plan

  1. Lunch: 100grams of protein
  2. Leaner protein is ideal, fish, chicken, ect.

Calorie deficit plan

  1. Lunch: 200g of protein, whole food, not shakes
  2. Leaner protein is ideal, fish, chicken, ect.

Maintenance plan

  1. Lunch: 100g of protein (easy to meal prep), higher-fat is ideal
  2. Dinner: Try to back fill in an additional 50-100g of protein, eat whole food carbs and clean fat, but this is also the meal to let life happen. Don't heavily scrutinize unless you are gaining weight consistently for more than two weeks.

Calorie surplus plan

  1. Breakfast: Aim for 20-30g of protein and some good carbs, eggs and cornmeal pan-cakes, or over-night oats are perfect.
  2. Lunch: 100g of protein (easy to meal prep), higher-fat is ideal
  3. Dinner: Try to back fill in an additional 50-100g of protein, eat whole food carbs and clean fat, but this is also the meal to let life happen. Don't heavily scrutinize unless you are gaining weight consistently for more than two weeks.
